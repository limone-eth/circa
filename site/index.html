hi, this is lumen.

a mac app that keeps your screen in sync with the sun.

full daylight color while the sun is up. then, as it sets wherever you are, your screen slowly warms to candlelight amber and gently dims — so evening screen time stops fighting your sleep.

it can also stop LED backlight flicker (PWM) for tired eyes.

all in native swift. open-source. all local. free. enjoy.
